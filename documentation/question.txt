# AI Engineering Technical Assessment

**Duration:** 60 minutes | **Format:** Live coding, no prep required | **API key:** Gemini (provided)

---

## The Task

The [Hacker News API](https://github.com/HackerNews/API) is a free, public, no-auth API that exposes the front page of Hacker News in real time.

**Your goal:** Fetch the current #1 story and its comments, then use an LLM to produce a structured analysis of the discussion.

You may retrieve the data however you like: programmatic fetch, curl, or copy-paste from the browser. What matters is what you do with it.

---

## Required Outputs

Produce a structured object that captures the following. The field design is up to you.

- **Summary:** what is this story about, based on the discussion?
- **Key themes:** the main topics the community is actually debating
- **Notable insights:** specific, well-supported claims or perspectives surfaced in the comments
- **Community sentiment:** the overall tone of the thread
- **Controversy signal:** how heated or divided is the discussion?
- **Reasoning trace:** your model's chain of thought before arriving at conclusions
